Welcome Jumbos!

Welcome to TuftScavenger!

The purpose of this app is to give prospective students and new Jumbos a better way to get to know and find their way around our campus.

Take part in a Scavenger Hunt around Tufts!

Using the Google Maps, TuftScavenger features a mobile-exclusive scavenger hunt consisting of a number of buildings and other landmarks around campus.

Study in other ways!

TuftScavenger is not just a mobile scavenger hunt app -- we're also Desktop-friendly! We have a number of quiz-style questions and flashcards to help you really get to know Tufts, as well an interactive map with locations listed on it so people on Desktop won't feel left out.
